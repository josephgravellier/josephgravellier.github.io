Publications Teaching Talks
List of courses
  • Introduction to attacks against a machine learning based system
    Given to second year engineering students of school "Ecole des Mines de Saint-Etienne"
    Content:
    - Overview of main threats targetting a machine learning system under the CIA (Confidentiality, Integrity, Availability) triad: poisoning attacks, membership inference attacks, reverse engineering attacks, adversarial examples.
    - Presentation of basic gradient-based and score-based attacks: FGSM, BIM, CWL2, ZOO and SPSA
    - Presentation of adversarial training and some of its variants to defend against adversarial examples
    - Presentation of Differential Privacy to defend against membership inference attacks

  • Algorithms and programmation
    Given to first year engineering students of school "Ecole des Mines de Saint-Etienne"
    Content:
    - Study and implementation of graph theory algorithms in C: Dijkstra, Bellman-Ford and Chandy-Misra
