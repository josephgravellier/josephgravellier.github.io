About me

I am currently completing a Ph.D. in statistics and computer science, under the supervision of Pierre-Alain Moellic and Jean-Max Dutertre.
My research focuses on the security of machine learning models, more specifically on developing defenses against adversarial examples.
Before that, I graduated from the engineering school ENSIIE (computer science, statistics and applied mathematics) and obtained a MSc degree in statistical learning from the Université Paris-Saclay.
My work is part of a cooperation between the CEA (French public reseach institute) and the SAS (Security of Architectures and Systems) research team of the engineering school "Ecole des Mines de Saint-Etienne".
